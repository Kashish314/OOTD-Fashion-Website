About Us

OOTD was started to solve a simple problem: Building a great wardrobe is hard.
We make it easier by connecting people with personal stylists online so that you can experience the best services at home.
As an Indian company, we combine top brands, expert service, and unparalleled convenience to help people build their best wardrobes.

Fast Facts

OUR MERCHANDISE

OOTD carries an expansive variety of high quality inventory for women including casual, business attire, formal wear, outerwear, shoes, bags, and select accessories at various price points to make a budget friendly outfit for you.


SIZING

Women's sizes range from petite to plus size, and OOTD also carries ready-to-wear apparel.This includes a full range of custom-tailored casual and business wear,including suits, trousers, outerwear, shirts, skirts,dresses etc.


STYLE ADVISORS

At OOTD, our stylists are real people invested in your personal style. All stylists go through extensive training and have hands-on experience to know which colors, sizes, styles, and brands will look and fit best on each customer.


What Makes Us Different?

We are not cut from the same cloth as other retailers. For starters, we're more about service than fashion. We don't push trends onto people. Instead, we connect with people one-on-one to find the clothes that fit their taste, lifestyle, and budget-and we do so with innovative methods that are unparalleled in the industry.

We value brick-and-mortar and digital experiences equally. Hosting customers for shopping appointments online to have a box of hand-selected clothing sent to their door, we uphold a level of service that earned us a place in the multi-cultural Indian families.
