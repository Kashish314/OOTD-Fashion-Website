REFUND & REPLACEMENT

We accept Returns of products incase if there is any unfortunate incident such as receiving your product DAMAGED, if you've received the wrong size or incase if you have received a wrong product by mischance.

In such a case, we request you to contact us Immediately within 7 days of receiving such product by writing an email to us at info@ootd.in with the details of the issue and attach relevant images in the email along with mentioning your Order Id. Please use the email id from which you had originally placed the order.

Depending on your situation, we will ensure that if you have received any Damaged or Wrong product, we will either issue you a Refund or Replacement, immediately after we receive the Product back from you. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

Our Customer Care team is at your service 24X7 and will guide you on how to return the Product to us. We ensure you that your queries are served our top priority.

CANCELLATION

With the nature of our Business, it is not possible to cancel any of your order once its been placed after the bookings have closed or shipped. However if you have made Duplicate orders by mistake, by all means, please write to us at info@ootd.in and we will be ready to help you.

Clothes, boxes, accessories, footwear or any other product, once purchased from us, can not be cancelled once the bookings have closed or the items have been shipped.


All Sales are Final

In extreme case and depending upon your situation, if the Cancellation is approved then their will be a Deduction of 5% which is a Non Refundable amount towards the Fees of Third Party Payment Gateway and the Website Subscription charges.

SHIPPING & DELIVERY TIMELINES


Shipping Charges

Shipping Charges depends on the Geographical Location all over India. We collaborate with the Best Logistics Supplier in the World to deliver your packages in a timely fashion. Our Shipping Partner automatically sends you the tracking id after the product is dispatched from our Fulfillment center, on your SMS and/or email.


The Shipping charges are minimal and is automatically calculated based on the shipping address. It varies as per weight and reciever's address.



International Shipping

As of now we do not ship anywhere apart from India.



Shipping/Dispatch TimeLine

All Products have different Shipping Timelines and they are usually mentioned on the same Product Page.


Please do check as it varies from product to product.


Readily available products from the Shops, which are NOT on Pre Orders are shipped within 7-9 days of placing the orders.

If however, you do not receive your Tracking details over the email, please write to info@ootd.in and you will be assisted promptly.
