How much does it costs?

Is there a monthly fee?

Nope, no monthly fee. Just skip the month if you’re not in need of any new clothes. Each box shipped has a ₹750 styling fee, which is credited toward any items you keep.

Will it fit my budget?

Our items range from ₹1000-₹10k. You can always preview and customize your box to make sure your box fits your budget.

When will I be charged?

You will be charged when we ship your box. Return any articles you don’t like for free, and you will be refunded entirely.
